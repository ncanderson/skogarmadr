Printer table
Welded metal supports with AB 3/4 plywood. Clear coat varnish for rust, rot, and scratch protection.
I was in a small dorm room, and needed a table for my printer, as well as a small work surface to expand my desk space, but I needed to keep the scanner lid free on the printer, and I didn't want to lose storage space. So I designed this. Ironically, by the time I finished making it, I had moved into an apartment. But it still works out pretty well.

Printer table (planning stage)
Pen on cardboard, Google SketchUp
The first pen sketch, then a 3D model to picture it more spatially. A few things changed (The metal was originally meant to be more plywood, and more support was added for better strength) but otherwise it remained fairly close to that original concept.
