Hero Sheet and Kentaur 2
Adobe Photoshop Elements + Bamboo tablet
These were concepts for another friend's untitlted video game project. This game was a Greek mythology action adventure kind of deal, so naturally it needed a classical-looking Greek hero and lots mythological beasties. These two show the progression of items/armor for the hero, and variation 2 of a series of Centaur concepts.

Sea Jump
Adobe Photoshop Elements + Bamboo tablet
From the same game as before, this piece was to illustrate an idea of an ability that the hero would pick up along the way. An idea we were talking about was that they were the child of Poseidon, and at some point in the game, Poseidon would grant them favors and abilities, like this jump extension.

Sir Joshua of Wedin
Adobe Photoshop Elements + Bamboo tablet
This was a commission to extrapolate the client's model "signature figure" into a full-size, realistic figure study. I used 14th century armor as reference for the armor details, but made sure they matched the figure's established styled and colors.
